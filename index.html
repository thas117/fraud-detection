How Our Fraud Detection Works

Our AI analyzes hundreds of data points to identify fraudulent transactions with 99.9% accuracy.

1

Transaction Monitoring

Every transaction is analyzed in real-time using our proprietary AI algorithms that examine hundreds of behavioral and contextual signals.

2

Risk Scoring

Each transaction receives a risk score based on factors like device fingerprinting, location data, purchase patterns, and more.

3

Decision Engine

Our system automatically approves, flags, or declines transactions based on customizable risk thresholds and business rules.

4

Continuous Learning

The AI model continuously improves as it processes more transactions and receives feedback on its decisions.
